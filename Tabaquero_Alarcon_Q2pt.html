The Elements

After they found a way to use magical powers by chemical bondings, The Periodic Company conducts multiple experiments on people willing to join, whether if they're rich or poor, it doesn't matter. Each people will have a unique power, based on a chemical bonding.

Characters

  • Mr. Heliulus - CEO of The Periodic Company. Based on what his employees said, he's quite nice, even making things "lighter". He leads the experiments.
  • The Compounders - Scientists who help Mr. Heliulus with experiments. They're the people that does the experiments.
  • Stevel - A subject that has been experimented with a ionic bond. Their elements are Iron and Carbon.
  • Haito - Another subject that been xperimented with a convalent bond. His elements are Hydrogen and Oxygen.
  • Chloe Sodius - An orphan girl that has a useless power in combat, so Mr. Heliulus decided to teach her on cooking for subjects and scientists to eat. Her elements are Sodium and Clorine.
